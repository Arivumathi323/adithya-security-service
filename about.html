About ADITHYA

Building trust through excellence in security and housekeeping services since 2010.

Our Story

14+ Years of Trusted Service

ADITHYA Security Services was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide professional, reliable, and ethical security solutions to businesses across Tamil Nadu.

What started as a small team of dedicated security professionals has grown into a comprehensive security and facility management company serving over 150 clients across 25 districts.

Our commitment to compliance, continuous training, and client satisfaction has made us the preferred security partner for leading corporations, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and industrial establishments.
