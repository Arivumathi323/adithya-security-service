Our Philosophy

Training That Makes a Difference

At ADITHYA, we believe that well-trained personnel are the foundation of excellent security service. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to equip our team with the skills, knowledge, and attitude needed to excel.

Every new recruit undergoes a rigorous 2-week induction program, followed by ongoing refresher training throughout their employment. Our training is conducted by experienced professionals, including former defense and police personnel.
